Aquascaping styles that we can create

The Nature Aquarium

This aquascaping style is characterized by attention to the alignment and grouping of hardscape. Rocks and driftwood are mixed with aquatic plants in order to create an underwater replica of a particular terrestrial landscape.

The Dutch Aquarium

The Dutch Aquarium style does not imply the use of driftwoods, rocks and other hardscape materials. The main focus is placed upon the growth and arrangement of aquatic plants.

Biotop Aquarium

The biotope style primary goal is to always create a scene that would be reflective of what could be naturally taking place in a river, swamp, or other aquatic terrain.
